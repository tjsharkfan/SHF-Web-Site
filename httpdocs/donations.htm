Your donation to the SHF is very important to us!

The Saratoga Historical Foundation is a tax-exempt 501c(3) organized under California statues. Financial contributions are greatly appreciated. Other donations cannot be accepted without review of the Curator and the Collections Committee. Space at the Museum is limited, and our collections policies discourage accepting material that does not have a Saratoga, California or West Valley connection.
