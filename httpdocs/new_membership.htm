Your membership in the SHF is very important to us!

We do not receive funding from federal, state, county, or city sources. We need your support for our activities and we rely on membership dues for our finances. We have some great activities and programs planned for this year and we hope you won't miss any of them! The SHF By-laws state that membership dues are to be paid by January 31 each year.

Renew or Become a Member Today!

The mission of the Saratoga Historical Foundation is to preserve the unique history of Saratoga for the education and enjoyment of the community. Your dues and contributions fund our annual operating budget and help keep the Saratoga History Museum open. As a member you'll be informed of the many educational and social activities associated with the museum.

Member Benefits:
  • 10% discount on many of the items at the Museum gift store
  • Free bi-monthly newsletter and email notification regarding key museum events
  • Reduced admission to all museum events
  • No admission charge to regularly scheduled member programs
  • Membership is tax deductible

    The Saratoga Historical Foundation is a tax-exempt 501c(3) organized under California statues. Financial contributions are greatly appreciated. Other donations cannot be accepted without review of the Curator and the Collections Committee. Space at the Museum is limited, and our collections policies discourage accepting material that does not have a Saratoga, California or West Valley connection.
