Saratoga Korean War Veterans


There are no local veterans who served in the Korean conflict that are known to have been killed in action. Saratoga residents who enlisted for the Korean War included the following seven youths who went through boot camp together. These seven friends enlisted in the Navy. They have been friends since grammar school and they are:

Jim Bailey
Jim Dresbach
Dave Duke
Don "Skip" Hall
Danny Kriege Lee
"Red" Parker
Terry Worden


Also recruited to serve in Korea from Saratoga were Jerry Caldwell and Rolin Langley.


Source: Files of the Saratoga Museum
April Halberstadt, May 2006
